LED Lighting: Frequently Asked Questions

Expert answers to your questions about LED lighting installation in the Antelope Valley.

How much does LED lighting installation cost?

It depends on the size of your home and the features you want. Basic security lighting starts around $1,200, while full smart seasonal systems start at $2,500. We offer free consultations and detailed quotes.

Can I change colors for different holidays?

Yes! Our smart seasonal systems use programmable RGB LEDs that you control from your phone. Switch from security white to Christmas red and green, Halloween orange and purple, or any custom color instantly.

Do you install security cameras too?

Absolutely. Our Complete Security Package includes both LED lighting and 4-8 professional security cameras with mobile app access. It's our most comprehensive offering for total home protection.

How long does installation take?

Most residential installations take 1-2 days depending on the size of your home and the complexity of the system. We'll give you an exact timeline during your free consultation.

Are LED lights energy efficient?

Yes! LEDs use up to 90% less energy than traditional bulbs and last 25+ years. Your system will pay for itself in energy savings while keeping your home safe and beautiful.

Do you service what you install?

Yes. We provide lifetime support on all our installations. If you ever have issues or want to upgrade, we're here to help. Andrew personally manages all service calls.
