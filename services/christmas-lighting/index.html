Christmas Lighting FAQs

Common questions about professional LED security lighting in the Antelope Valley.

How bright are security lights?

We customize brightness based on your needs. Typical perimeter lights range from 800-1200 lumens (bright enough to deter intruders without disturbing neighbors). Motion-activated lights can be brighter for maximum visibility when triggered.

Will the lights work during power outages?

Standard systems require power. However, we can integrate battery backup or solar options for critical areas if you want lights to function during outages. Discuss your needs during the consultation.

Can I control the lights remotely?

Yes! Our smart systems include mobile app control. Turn lights on/off, adjust brightness, set schedules, and monitor status from anywhere in the world.

How long do LED security lights last?

Quality LEDs last 25,000-50,000 hours (20-25+ years of typical use). They're also more durable than traditional bulbs and perform better in extreme High Desert temperatures.

Will security lighting increase my electric bill?

LEDs use up to 90% less energy than traditional bulbs. Most homeowners see minimal impact on their electric billsâ€”often just $5-15 per month for a full perimeter system.

Do you service what you install?

Absolutely. We provide lifetime support on all installations. If you ever have issues, want to add lights, or need adjustments, Andrew personally manages all service calls.
