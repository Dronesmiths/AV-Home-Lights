The Founder's Story

Rooted in Reliability

AV Home Lighting began with a clear mission: to provide the Antelope Valley with professional LED lighting that prioritizes safety, energy efficiency, and beautiful illumination. We saw too many properties with outdated, inefficient lighting and decided to set a higher standard.

We've grown into the region's premier choice for security lighting, holiday displays, and landscape illumination. We understand the High Desert climate and design systems built to withstand extreme temperatures and harsh conditions.

Our team is trained, licensed, and safety-focused. From the initial design consultation to the final installation, we deliver expert LED lighting solutions with professional care.
